Top items.

What makes your IF collection great? The rare and hard to find items. These are the ones that don't come by too often. Everyone wants them. Or maybe they are just cool.

This list is merely an opinion and open to suggestions from other collectors. I look forward to your input.

15- Black-Ash Inheritance Shaped CD

Not exactly hard to find but certainly one of the coolest In Flames releases from the earlier days. Shaped like a Jesterhead, this CD is a classic release and collectors item for In Flames fans and collectors alike.

  

14- A Sense Of Purpose Mail Order Edition

Limited to 1500 copies, this is the third 'mail order edition'. A working labyrinth much like the classic child toy. Comes with a numbered certificate. Even the balls have Jesterheads on them!

     

13- Subterranean 12" Picture Disc

Limited to 1000 copies.

  

12- Lunar Strain 12" Picture Disc

Limited to 1000 copies.

  

11- Subterranean 12"

Limited to 1000 copies.

  

10- Lunar Strain 12"

Limited to 1000 copies.

   

9- Whoracle 12"

Made in  Germany. This hard to find 12" vinyl contains the vinyl only track "Re-Cycles" which is what makes it highly sought after. But is it really a rare track? The song is easy to find under its proper name but this is the only place where you can get it on vinyl.

8- Come Clarity Mail Order Edition

The second of the 'mail order editions', this clear acrylic cube is highly sought after. Comes with a numbered certificate and clear 'cell like' artwork.

     

7- Soundtrack To Your Escape Mail Order Edition

The first of the 'mail order editions', this wooden box comes with a numbered certificate and over sized wooden matches.

     

6- Clayman - Shaped CD

Limited to 1000 copies. This CD is just cool. Cover actual warns you not to play this in a car stereo.

  

5- Black-Ash Inheritance - Shaped and Hand Numbered

Limited to 555 copies and hand numbered. Still someone what easy to find but in 5 years will a real treasure.

  

4- Bullet Ride Promo (USA)

This rare CD is possibly the first real USA promotional CD. Contains 3 tracks and doesn't show up that often.

  

3- Take This Life/Leeches 7" Promo (USA)

Limited to 500 copies, this clear 7" vinyl is one rare promo and possibly the first ever In Flames 7".

  

2- Black Ash Inheritance - UNCUT 12"

Limited to possibly 10-20 copies. The actual amount is actually unknown. By far the second hardest item to obtain and the rarest of all vinyls.

  

1- In Flames Demo '93 - Promo Cassette

The earliest In Flames release. Made by the band themselves and passed around. Good luck finding one. This is the number one piece. 190 items later and I'm still searchin'...
