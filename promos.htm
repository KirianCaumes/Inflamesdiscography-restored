Promotional Releases

Highly collectible, promotional releases cannot be bought in stores. Often given away to radio stations and DJ's to promote up coming albums. Sometimes they include exclusive rare tracks, may be edited and/or contain alternate artwork.

Cassette

Promo Demo 1993

The first ever In Flames release, and the rarest item in my opinion. This cassette was self made by the band to promote awareness and help in finding a record deal. Apparently there are 250 copies out there. Can anyone confirm this?

 

Thanks to Concuer from Jesterhead.com I appreciate the scan!

Vinyl

Take This Life/Leeches  FPRO7-7 (USA) Ferret Music

Clear 7" vinyl. Limited to 500 copies. This is one rare item. It is the FIRST ever official 7" vinyl by In Flames. It should be noted that this vinyl was originally part of the 'Ferret Fan Pack'.

CD - various artists, splits and teasers

Reroute To Remain/Meshuggah (USA) Nuclear Blast America

Four Track Split two from In Flames and Meshuggah.

   

 

Soundtrack To Your Escape Media Market Teaser CD (Germany) Nuclear Blast WANTED

  

 

Soundtrack To Your Escape Sampler (USA) Nuclear Blast America

8 Track split sampler  featuring In Flames, MNEMIC, Raunchy and Ektomorf.

  

 

Come Clarity US Tour - 2006 CD Sampler FPRO8-2 (USA) Ferret Music

Contains 11 tracks. Features Take This Life & Leeches from In Flames. Other bands include Trivium, Devildrive, Zao and bonus tracks from Every Time I Die, A Life Once Lost and Scarlet. Note: This CD was issued without inserts.

Metal Edge May 2008  (USA) Metal Edge Magazine

On the news stands for one month only. The May 2008 issue's collector CD bares In Flames artwork as they were the editor's pick for that month. Makes for a unique collectible and one hard to find pressing that contains The Mirror's Truth.
