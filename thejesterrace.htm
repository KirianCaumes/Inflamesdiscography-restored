The Jester Race ~ Black-Ash Inheritance

Originally released in 2002, this reissue adds the out of print Black-Ash Inheritance EP to the classic In Flames album.

Promo

Thailand NB 616-2 Platinum Marketing under license to Nuclear Blast

     

CD

USA 6616-2 Nuclear Blast America

Unlike the Germany pressing, the USA version comes in a jewel case.

  

 

USA NB 2204-2 Nuclear Blast America

   

 

Germany  NB 616-2 Nuclear Blast

Unlike the USA pressing, the Germany version comes in a digipak. The back also lists an 1996 copyright while the USA lists 2002.

  

 

Germany NB 2204-2 Nuclear Blast

Reloaded Edition

    

 

Brazil 55616-2 Nuclear Blast distributed by Century Media Records Ltda.

     

 

Mexico WOE005 Scarecrow Records

The Mexico version of 'The Jester Race ~ Black-Ash Inheritance' is the only version to be given a grey border that says deluxe edition which is similiar to the USA/Germany deluxe editions of Whoracle, Colony and Clayman.
