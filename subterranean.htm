Subterranean -released in 1995

Test Pressing

12" While Label vinyl with info sheet.

This particular test pressing is for the upcoming reissue by Back On Black. The labels for the vinyl say Side A and have a hand written 'B' for the b-side.

  

Promo

Japan TFCK-87311 Toy's Factory

With promotional stamps. Disc reads 'sample/loaned'.

  

Vinyl

Sweden RR LP002 Regain Records

12", Limited to 1000 copies.

     

  

 

Sweden RR PLP002 Regain Records

12" Picture Disc, Limited to 1000 copies.

   

 

UK  BOBV188LP Back On Black

12" coloured vinyl reissue. Manufactured and distributed by Plastic Head.

First ever pressing of Subterranean. All other versions came after.
