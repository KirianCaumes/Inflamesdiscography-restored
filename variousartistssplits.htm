Various Artists and Splits

In Flames random appearances.

It should be noted that I am only listing what I am after. That is most promotional videos, some promotional CD's and any commercial various CD's that contain non album tracks.

Promo

Nuclear Blast Records Presents  Nuclear Blast America (USA)

VHS with five videos including My Sweet Shadow from In Flames.

  

 

Nuclear Blast Records Presents Nuclear Blast America (USA)

Digital Video Cassette with five videos including My Sweet Shadow from In Flames.

 

Sounds Of The Underground 2006- Live & Rare  (USA)

Contains 'Come Clarity (Remix)' previously unreleased. The only other pressing to contain this very track is the USA Come Clarity promo.

  

 

Metal Edge May 2008 (USA) Metal Edge Magazine

On the news stands for one month only. The May 2008 issue's collector CD bares In Flames artwork as they were the editor's pick for that month. Makes for a unique collectible and one hard to find pressing that contains The Mirror's Truth.

  

 

KOCH Records October 2008 New Release Sampler KOCH Records (USA)

A rare sampler CD containing In Flames 'Alias'. The song was just made a single around this time.

  

CD

Bam Margera presents Viva La Bands Volume Two F086-2 Ferret Music

Contains an earlier version of 'Abnegation'. The newer version can be found on 'The Mirror's Truth' EP.

  

Video

Live & Plugged NB 262-3 Nuclear Blast

VHS split with Darkseed and Evereve. Back insert lists the catalogue number as NB 256-3 while the spine lists it as NB 262-3. The latter makes more sense as it matches the bar code number.
