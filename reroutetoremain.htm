CD

Japan TFCK-87289 Toy's Factory

1st Press  With slip cover and 6 post cards. The sticker on the shrinkwrap acts as the 'OBI' and it should be noted that copies without it are incomplete.

     

      

     

 

2nd Press Without slip cover and post cards.

  

 

Japan  QATE 10002  Trooper

  


Germany NB 624-0 Nuclear Blast

Digipak with alternate artwork.

  

 

Germany NB 624-2 Nuclear Blast

1st Press  Copyright info on the back insert if off center and to the right. Catalogue number on the disc is incorrect. The disc reads NB 1058-2 which is from the 4 track teaser promotional CD and not 624-2 which is the correct catalogue number. The artwork colour is also a lighter shade of green. Later pressings are darker.

     

 

2nd Press  Copyright info is properly centered and the catalogue number on the disc is corrected. The artwork on the disc is a darker green.

     

 

Germany Nuclear Blast A Myth?
