Slipcase with cardboard pullout and DVD.  Spelling error on the back lists the Jesterhead fan club as 'Festerhead'.

     

        

     

Below is the insert that came from the Newburycomics preorder. Mine originally came with four autographs. I had the chance to obtain the final autograph at the meet and greet on April 29th, 2008. Anders was the missing signature.

Clean 'Edited' version. Spelling error on the back insert lists the Jesterhead fan club as 'Festerhead'.

   

 

USA D173240 BMG

Record club issue from BMG is almost identical to the standard USA issue with the exception of the BMG catalogue number being printed on the last page of the front insert. The 'Festerhead' error is also included on the back insert.

Germany  NB-2083-5 Nuclear Blast

Mail Order Edition Unlike the previous Mail Order Editions, the Labyrinth is limited to 1500 copies. Comes with two balls that actually have Jesterheads printed on them!  Too cool. Contains the DVD, CD and a certificate from Nuclear Blast.

     

     

     

 

Germany NB 2255-2 Nuclear Blast
