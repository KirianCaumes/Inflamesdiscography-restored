The Top 21

Every band has it's 'Holy Grail' of rare and hard to find items. It is my opinion that these are the hardest to find.

Of course, there are some exceptions. Some items can be rare but easier to find if you know where to look, such as the Black-Ash Inheritance Shaped CD.

Other items, like the Uncut Black-Ash Inheritance vinyl or CD are by far the hardest for any collector to find and thus sought after. When they do show up, they are often sold at ridiculous prices.

Below you will find, in my opinion, the top 21 In Flames items. And stay tuned, as this list may grow over time. There are always undiscovered items to be found, and I value your imput.

Note: Test Pressings are not included in this list. It is well known that Test Pressings exist in even fewer quantities than the items listed here. I have left them out as I find they are rather bland to look at. I will be doing a Test Pressing page soon with the help of a friend.

Top Range Rarities (Items 1 - 8)

The Holy Grail of In Flames collecting with  both Uncut releases as the rarest of all to date.

1- Promo-Demo 1993. Apparently 250 self made copies by the original lineup!

 

2- Black-Ash Inheritance Uncut 12" vinyl. Possibly 10-20 in existence.

  

 

3- Black-Ash Inheritance Uncut CD

   

 

4- Come Clarity USA Mispressed - Plays one song - Come Clarity. Two known copies in existence

    

 

5- A Sense Of Purpose - Japan Advance Promo

 

 

6- Take This Life USA 7" Promo. Limited to 500 copies.

    

 

7- Black-Ash Inheritance Shaped 10", Limited to 555 Copies, Hand numbered

  

 

8- Bullet Ride USA Promo

  

Mid Range Rarities (Items 9 - 17)

Still hard to find, just not as hard as the above items.  Though that Whoracle vinyl can be pricey hehe.

9- Clayman Germany Shaped Sawblade CD, Limited to 1000 Copies

       

 

10- Soundtrack To Your Escape Mail Order Edition, Limited to 1000 Copies

       

 

11- Come Clarity Mail Order Edition, Limited to 1000 Copies

     

 

12- Whoracle Germany 12" Vinyl

     

     

 

13- Reroute To Remain 4 Track teaser in Envelope

      

        

 

14- Lunar Strain 12", Limited to 1000 Copies

     

     

 

15- Subterranean 12", Limited to 1000 Copies

     

    

 

16- Lunar Strain 12" Picture Disc, Limited to 1000 Copies

    

 

17- Subterranean 12" Picture Disc, Limited to 1000 Copies

   

Bottom Range Rarities (Items 18 - 21)

Much more common than the above items. Some of these are just classic in the In Flames collecting world, such as the Shaped Black-Ash Inheritance CD. I've included the Korean pressing of Come Clarity as the Paper Dolls do NOT come inside the packaging and many sellers are not aware of this.

18- Come Clarity, Korea w/Paper Doll Set

   

    

 

19- Cloud Connected Germany CD Single

    

 

20- Black Ash Inheritance Germany Shaped CD

  

 

21- A Sense Of Purpose Mail Order Edition, Limited to 1500 Copies
