Bootlegs

Some might call them miscellaneous items. Some are just odd. These are the items I have collected that don't seem to fit anywhere. The bottom line is, the band hasn't authorised these releases in any way and sometimes contain some unreleased tracks or rare live recordings.

Vinyl

In Flames Performing Live At The Fillmore Theater

Supposedly limited to 10 copies worldwide! I have my doubts. Both sleeve and label misspell 'Moonshield' as 'Moonsheild'.

     

CD

Metall Empire Hi-End Ultra records

2CD compilation. Says made in the EU on the back.

  

 

Live In Flames 3  UFD-041 Unreleased File Death

2 CD showcasing In Flames performance from 13/11/00 in Osaka, Japan while on the Clayman tour.  Bonus tracks are from The Haunted and Armageddon. 

While the back says 'For promotional use only, not for sale' and it is numbered, do not be fooled.

  

 

Destroys SRF 2004  HOK-016  House Of Kicks

Hand numbered and limited to 666 copies. How Metal :) Thanks to Sebbe 'Third Eye" Carlsson. Many thanks, my friend

The track listing is improperly listed on the back insert. Track 8 is actually Cloud Connected while track 10 is Clayman. Only For The Weak is not on the album at all.

     

 

Beast To Remain  SP-121902391 Sylph WANTED

Thanks to Dredmann from the Jesterhead for sharing this discovery.  This bootleg features two complete shows that were recorded in Japan 2002.

  

MP3 CD

In Flames MP3 Collection RMG Records

Made in Russia, contains The Jester Race, Whoracle, Colony, Clayman, The Tokyo Showdown, Reroute To Remain, Trigger, The Quiet Place and Soundtrack To Your Escape all one on disc. Also contains mp3 software player, slideshow and lyrics. Crazy!

 

In Flames MP3DM-256/1617-5 Star Records

Made in Russia. Seems to be an updated version of the above release. Contains all the albums plus two live bootlegs - Popstad Gothenburg and Live in Gothenburg. Also contains previously unreleased In Flames material. More info to come!

  

  

Cassette

The Quiet Place FE4805 FE Record

14 track compilation with two live tracks.
