CD

Germany NB 1231-0 Nuclear Blast.

Digipack with alternate cover art and bonus track 'Discover Me Like Emptiness'. 

  

 

Germany NB 1231-2 Nuclear Blast

   

 

Germany NB 1231-5 Nuclear Blast

Simply the Digipak edition (NB 1231-0) shrink-wrapped with the DVD (NB 1445-2). An insert is packaged with both releases and given its own catalogue number of NB 1231-5 to show they came together.  Otherwise, it is just two separate releases. The insert is proof they came together as is the CD + DVD sticker on the cover/shrink-wrap.

   

 

Germany  Nuclear Blast

Mail Order Edition Limited to 1000 copies and available by mail order only. Contains the album with two bonus tracks - Discover Me Like Emptiness and Clayman Live Wacken 2003 as well as a DVD featuring In Flames Live In Korea 2002.
