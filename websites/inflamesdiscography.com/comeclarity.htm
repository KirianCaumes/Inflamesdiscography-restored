Ferret Fan Pack

Limited to 500 copies, the Ferret Fan Pack contains the USA Digipak (F962) version of the CD, a shirt, patch and a very cool clear 7" promotional vinyl which was only available in this order. The vinyl is the true gem in my opinion.

Germany Mail Order Edition Nuclear Blast

Limited to 1000 copies, the album is split onto TWO CD's. Also contains a CD-ROM, DVD and some clear "film cell" style artwork with a numbered certificate. The cell which lists the tracking listings has the working title for Versus Terminus - End Of Things. The CD-Rom portion misspells Take This Life as Take This Live.
