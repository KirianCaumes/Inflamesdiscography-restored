Russia

Bootlegged version. Looks almost identical to the 1st Pressing from Germany but there are a few differences that let us tell them apart. First, there is no image under the CD tray. It is absolutely blank unlike the Flaming Jesterhead seen on the 1st Press from Germany. Seconly, the backside of this CD lacks a Matrix number.

     

 

Taiwan TFCK-87214 Magnum

Made in Japan, given an OBI and exported to Taiwan. Aside from the OBI, it appears almoist identical to the Japan 1st press and does include the 'Heavy Metal Family Tree'. Should be regarded as the 1st press for Taiwan.

  

 

Taiwan NB 2207-2 Magnum

Made in Germany, shipped to Taiwan and given a Taiwan OBI. Bares the same catalogue number as the Germany Reloaded edition and contains all of the same content. Should be regarded as the 2nd press for Taiwan.
