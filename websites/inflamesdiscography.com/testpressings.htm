Test Pressings

Test Pressings are by far the RAREST of In Flames items. According to wikipedia, Test Pressings are produced in limited quantities (usually under 5) and used to evaluate the the quality of the disc production.

So why aren't they in the Top 21, you are asking? Well, these items are in a league all of their own. And I also find them quite plain and boring to look at. But, this site is intended to showcase all that is out there. So I give you a list of In Flames Test Pressings. Hopefully we can track all of these down.

A HUGE thank you to Chris Heinke for supplying the majority of the pictures. Those Matrix shots looked hard to get. I'm also jealous :)
