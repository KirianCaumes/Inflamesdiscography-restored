Lunar Strain -released in 1994

Test Pressing

Double white label 12" with info sheet.

This particular test pressing is for the upcoming reissue by Back On Black. The sleeves are taped together and contain a sticker with the catalogue number. The labels for both vinyls say Side A and have a hand written 'B' on them though the matrix numbers list the sides correctly in an A, B, C, D fashion.
