Used & Abused... In Live We Trust -released in 2005

First ever official DVD release. Be on the look out for the limited edition version that also contains two CD's as well as two DVD's. Sadly, they didn't include the Pantera Fucking Hostile cover on the CD's. Perhaps royalties were an issue?
