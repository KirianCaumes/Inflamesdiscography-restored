Black-Ash Inheritance - released in 1997

Vinyl

Italy NIGHT 009-SH Night Of The Vinyl Dead

Shaped 10"  Hand numbered and limited to 555 copies.

  

 

Uncut 12"  20 copies made. Or should I say, uncut? hehe

The above vinyl is cut down to 10"s with the shaped part exceeding 10"s while this one remains a complete 'uncut' 12"s. A rarity indeed and my opinion on the second rarest In Flames item out there.  The uncut version DID NOT come with an insert.

  

 

Promo Pack 

20 copies made with 5 reserved for employees of Night Of The Living Dead.

Included in this pack are the following:

- white cardboard folder with red In Flames logo and NOTVD embossed logo
- Black-Ash Inheritance Shaped picture disc (Not the testpress)
- In Flames glow-in-the-dark slipmat
- In Flames promo picture
- test pressing of the backing-card front side (other side is blank)
- test pressing of the backing-card back side (other side is blank)
- Unused Black-Ash sticker

CD

Germany NB 27361 62512 Nuclear Blast

Shaped CD

  

 

Uncut CD   

Unlike the above copy, this version was not available in stores. Certainly a real gem in the In Flames collecting world.  The Matrix code matches that of the cut 'Shaped' release which tells me this is 100% genuine.

   

Cassette

Poland MORBID NOIZZ 212 Morbid Noizz Productions WANTED

Amorphis/In Flames split.

Features 'Black-Ash Inheritance' from In Flames and 'My Kantele' by Amorphis. The J-card has Amorphis artwork on one side and In Flames on the other as sween below.
