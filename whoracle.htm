Vinyl

Germany NB 284-1 Nuclear Blast

12" Vinyl.

Contains the vinyl only track Re-Cycles. This song is actualy the Acoustic Medley found on the Black-Ash Inheritance shaped release. It is not some long lost unheard song people are searching for. 

I tend to believe it is called Re-Cycles for two reasons. They re-used the Acoustic Medley from the Black-Ash Inheritance Shaped release and the Acoustic Medley is also comprised of melodies of previous In Flames songs. These include Artifacts Of The Black Rain, Dead Eternity and Jotun. It's been 'recyled'. Clever bunch.

CD

USA NB 284-2 Nuclear Blast America

1st Press  Spine reads NBA 27361 62842 while the back insert reads NB 284-2. The front insert misprints the lyrics for Episode 666 as the lyrics for Morphing Into Primal are printed in its place. The front insert is also of a harder styled paper than the Germany copies which also contain the lyric misprint on early pressings.

Update Dec. 8th 2009- Discovered the original Nuclear Blast America flyer which came with some early releases.
